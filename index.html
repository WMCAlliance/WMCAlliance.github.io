Rules & Guidelines

General Gameplay
  1. Respect both staff and other players.
  2. No griefing or stealing.
  3. PvP is not allowed in Survival, including setting players on fire or breaking blocks below them.
  4. When joining a player-run town, be sure to protect your home. Issues should be resolved with the town owner, unless it breaks server rules.
  5. Hacks, game modifications (mods), or texture packs that provide advantages are never allowed.
  6. Staff positions (for us, op = admin) are not given out freely. Asking (or spamming) staff to give you such a rank will earn either a mute or a ban - sometimes permanent.
  7. Dirty or offensive skins are not allowed
Chat & Communications
  1. No advertising of other Minecraft servers (or similar).
  2. No spamming. This could mean a number of things; don't repeat yourself, don't repeatedly ask for things, don't send multiple /trade or /tpr requests, when correcting yourself only correct one word instead of repeating the entire sentence, etc.
  3. Keep swearing minimal. This is a family-friendly server
  4. Anything not allowed in regular chat is also not allowed in private messaging (also known as /msg, /tell or /whisper).
Accounts
  1. No account sharing - keep your account safe and secure, don't allow others to use it
  2. We ban the account not the player - if a friend used your account and got banned, it's your fault for not keeping it secure
  3. Unban requests should be submitted via Discord, not through secondary/friend game accounts
Expectations
  • If you notice a glitch that affects gameplay (or that causes cheating) then notify a staff member (either in chat or on the forums) as soon as possible.
  • If you cause accidental grief to another player, apologise and fix the issue immediately. If you cannot fix it yourself, contact an admin.
  • All players are expected to check back on this webpage for changes. Failure to do so could result in a ban you did not expect.
Discord Server
  • When joining, you should use a nickname that closely resembles your in-game name.
  • Impersonating another player by name will result in you getting an in-game ban
  • If you are banned in-game, you are welcome to try and appeal the ban within Discord - ask in the #ask-the-mods channel
  • If you require someone's attention, do not spam their name or private messages. Call for them once and wait for them to respond
  • Using all-caps is considered spam and will not be tolerated.
  • In-game rules still apply here. No swearing, abuse or spamming
